Task Management

The Autospaces Task Manager allows you to create reports—such as contracts in-house, incoming loan applications under consideration, buybacks, overdue title documents—and communicate about those report items with employees as well as dealers.

Reports items of interest are assigned to employees that must handle those data items. Employees are then notified of their assigned tasks when they log into the system.
